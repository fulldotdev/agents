---
name: prepare-call
description: Interview the user about an upcoming call until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to prepare a call or mentions "prepare me".
---

Inerview me about what the core purpose of the call is. Who will be attending the call? What are the goals you want to achieve from this call? The end goal is a list of recommended topics and questions for the call. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring existing context, urls, or whatever, do that first.
